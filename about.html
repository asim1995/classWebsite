About Us

It is the best tuition or Classes in the world. It is the most user-friendly ERP system in education industry. SmartClasses Mobile Application will help Tuition Classes (Coaching center), Small Institutes and Private Teachers to manage their various tedious tasks like Inquiry, Student Management, Attendance, Fee collection, Fee reminder, exam management, SMS process very efficiently.

We have researched the market and tried to find the software that will cater to the needs of the small and medium sized education centers, but did not find any. As of now most of the centers try to manage all their data via excel sheets and any harddisk crash is a nightmare for them. Through Smartclasses.in we have tried to introduce a simplest management software, keeping in mind almost all the daily needs of the classes.

We have made sure the software should not become a financial burden so have kept the price to the lowest possible.

Key Features

1
Auto SMS
Automatically send SMS alerts to parents when a student reaches or leaves the institute with help of RFID/Biometric readers.
2
Weekly Test
Conduct on-line tests based on MCQs for students. Give instant results and solutions without creating question bank.
3
Multi-Lang SMS
Send SMS alerts for Lectures, Attendance, Test schedule, Marks scored etc. in the mother-tongue of each student/parent
4
Email Alerts
Send Report card and other communication by Email to Students and Parents.. Supports bulk messaging with Templates and mail-merge facility.

What We Offer

1
Lecture Management
Generate full time-table chart for lectures. Follow lecture-wise attendance, schedule changes. Extra classes. Send bulk SMS to staff, students & parents.
2
Enquiry Management
Manage your enquiries properly with the help follow-up details. Follow it as hot/warm/ cold. Preserve follow up details like resource, cost per enquiry progress, conversion etc.
3
Contact management
Track a record of CVs received, other contacts, and business loop. Send bulk massages for special occasions and Festivals.
4
Syllabus Management
Arrange chapter-wise lecture schedule with time required for the entire topic. Track record for the actual time management according to the given schedule. Also have the details of faculty performance.
